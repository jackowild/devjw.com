Getting started with the HyperDeck Controller for Mac


  1. First, ensure you have the following:
    • An Apple Mac computer running OS X Lion (10.7).
    • A Blackmagic Design HyperDeck Studio or Studio Pro with firmware version 4.1.

  2. Connect your HyperDeck to your network or directly to your Mac via Ethernet.

  3. Set the IP address of your HyperDeck using the display menu directly on the device.
    To do this press the "Display" button → Navigate to "Setup" → "Ethernet" → "IP".

  4. Ensure your Mac is connected to the same network as your HyperDeck.

  5. Finally open the HyperDeck Controller application, enter the IP address of your HyperDeck and hit connect.

If you have any problems please get in touch at help@devjw.com.
